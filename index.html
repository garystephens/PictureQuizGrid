Gary's Picture Quiz Grid Generator

  1. Save up to 24 images for your quiz on your device
    Tip: Images will be automatically cropped to a square shape. Make sure that what's important in the image is roughly in the centre of the image, or that the image is roughly square in shape to being with.
  2. Choose all the images with this button ->
  3. Optionally set watermark text: Vertical
    On some devices, you can drag the watermark to your preferred position
  4. Take a screenshot of the grid (e.g. on Windows, you could use Greenshot)
    Some browsers give better image quality than others, e.g. on Windows, Firefox is better than Chrome.
