Gary's Picture Quiz Grid Generator

Easily create a picture quiz of up to 24 pictures, displayed in a numbered 4-column grid.

Simply select your images to upload...


Maximum 24 files


You can also crop/uncrop images individually by clicking on them.

Watermark text (optional):

Watermark can be dragged to your preferred location.


Instructions

  1. Save up to 24 images for your quiz on your device

    Tip: Images that are square in shape, or where the important part of the image is in the centre, work best.

  2. Press the button to upload your image files
  3. Optionally, type some watermark text to be overlaid on the grid
  4. Take a screenshot of the grid (resize the browser window to change the grid to a suitable size)
    OR
    to a PDF or to paper (you may need to adjust the browser window width first so that the grid fits onto the page, but if you do so then you may need to also adjust the position of the watermark).
