Gary's Picture Quiz Grid Generator

  1. Save up to 24 images for your quiz on your device

    Tip: Images that are square in shape, or where the important part of the image is in the centre, work best.

  2. Choose all the image files with this button ->
  3. Optionally, type some watermark text to be overlaid on the grid: and drag the watermark to your preferred position     Display watermark vertically
  4. Take a screenshot of the grid.
    Or print to a PDF or to paper (you may need to adjust the browser window width first so that the grid fits onto the page, but if you do so, and you have set watermark text, you'll also need to check the position of the watermark text).
