Gary's Picture Quiz Grid Generator

Easily create a picture quiz of up to 24 images, displayed in a numbered 4-column grid.

Simply select your images to upload...


Maximum 24 files


You can also crop/uncrop images individually by clicking on them.

Watermark text (optional):

Watermark can be dragged to your preferred location.


Instructions

  1. Save up to 24 images for your quiz on your device (making sure, of course, that you have the right to use the images)

    Tip: Images that are square in shape, or where the important part of the image is in the centre, work best.

  2. Press the button to upload your image files
  3. Optionally, type some watermark text to be overlaid on the grid
  4. Take a screenshot of the grid
    OR
    to paper or a PDF

    Note: Before making a screenshot or printing, you may need to resize the browser window to change the grid to a suitable size, and also adjust the position of any watermark you set.
