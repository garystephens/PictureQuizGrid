Gary's Picture Quiz Grid Generator

  1. Save up to 24 images for your quiz on your device

    Tip: Images will be automatically cropped to a square shape, so make sure that what's important in the image is roughly in the centre of the image, or that the image is roughly square in shape to begin with.

  2. Choose all the image files with this button ->
  3. Optionally, type some watermark text to be overlaid on the grid:     Display watermark vertically

    Note: You can drag the watermark to your preferred position

  4. Take a screenshot of the grid. Or print to a PDF or to paper (you may need to adjust the browser window width first, so that the grid fits onto the page, but if you do so, and you have set watermark text, you'll also need to check the position of the watermark text).
